Overview

For those that haven’t played before, the idea is to pick one team each day to advance in the tournament. If they win you move on, if they lose you’re out - no buying back in. Once you’ve picked a team, you cannot pick them again. If you run out of teams to pick, you are considered out if there are still others alive. Winner takes all if there is no tie. In the event of a tie, the pot will be split up by adding up the seeds of the correct picks.

How to Play

  • Sign Up using the link above, using your full name, email address, and phone number.
  • Entry fee is $5, which is due by 3/20 at noon. My Venmo is @David-McQuillen . If you don't have Venmo, shoot me a text.
  • Make your picks using the link above. When submitting, your full name and email address must match the info you entered when signing up (an error message will pop up if it doesn't).
  • You may change your pick as many times as you want up until the tipoff of the first game that day.
  • Once a pick has been made, you will see "Pick Submitted" next to your name in the Standings. After the cutoff time for each day, the final picks for the entire pool will be revealed.
  • If you do not submit a pick before the lock time, you are eliminated.
  • Follow the pool results at the "Standings" link above.

Standings Display

  • Before a day's reveal time, standings show Pick Submitted or No Pick.
  • After the reveal time, the actual team picked will appear.
  • Winning picks are shown in green.
  • Losing picks are shown in red.
  • Once a player is eliminated, later days are blacked out.
