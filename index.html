NCAA Tournament Survivor 2026

How It Works

For those that haven’t played before, the idea is to pick one team each day to advance in the tournament. If they win you move on, if they lose you’re out - no buying back in. Once you’ve picked a team, you cannot pick them again. If you run out of teams to pick, you are considered out if there are still others alive. Winner takes all if there is no tie. In the event of a tie, the pot will be split up by adding up the seeds of the correct picks. For a two-way tie where Player 1 picked two #1 seeds and two #2 seeds, and Player 2 picked three #1 seeds and a #6 seed, the split would be 40% to Player 1 and 60% to Player 2.

Basic Rules

  • You may pick one team per day.
  • You cannot use the same team twice.
  • You may change your pick anytime before the lock time.
  • If your team loses, you are eliminated.
  • If you miss a pick before lock time, you are eliminated.

Website Pages

  • Home: Pool rules and instructions.
  • Standings: View all players and their progress by day.
  • Make a Pick: Submit or change your pick before the lock time.

Standings Display

  • Before a day's reveal time, standings show Pick Submitted or No Pick.
  • After the reveal time, the actual team picked will appear.
  • Winning picks are shown in green.
  • Losing picks are shown in red.
  • Once a player is eliminated, later days are blacked out.

How to Submit a Pick

  1. Go to Make a Pick.
  2. Enter your name exactly the same way each time.
  3. Select the day you want to make a pick for.
  4. Select an available team.
  5. Click Submit Pick.
