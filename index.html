Welcome to the March Madness Survivor Pool

How It Works

In this pool, each player selects one team per game day. If your team wins, you survive and move on. If your team loses, or if you fail to submit a pick before the deadline, you are eliminated.

Basic Rules

  • You may pick only one team for each day of games.
  • You cannot use the same team more than once during the tournament.
  • You may change your pick as many times as you want before that day's picks lock.
  • Once picks lock for a day, no more changes are allowed for that day.
  • If your selected team loses, you are eliminated.
  • If you do not submit a pick before the lock time, you are eliminated.

Website Pages

  • Home: Pool rules and instructions.
  • Standings: View all players and their progress by day.
  • Make a Pick: Submit or change your pick before the lock time.

Standings Display

  • Before a day's picks are revealed, standings will show Pick Submitted or No Pick.
  • After the day's reveal time, the actual team picked will appear.
  • Winning picks are shown in green.
  • Losing picks are shown in red.
  • Once a player is eliminated, later days are blacked out.

How to Submit a Pick

  1. Go to Make a Pick.
  2. Enter your name exactly the same way each time.
  3. Select the day you want to make a pick for.
  4. Select an available team.
  5. Click Submit Pick.
