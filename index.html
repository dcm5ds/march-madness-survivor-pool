2025 NCAA March Madness Survivor Pool

How to Play

For those that haven’t played before, the idea is to pick one team each day to advance in the tournament. If they win, you move on; if they lose, you’re out—no buying back in.

Once you’ve picked a team, you cannot pick them again. If you run out of teams to pick, you are considered out if there are still others alive.

Winner takes all if there is no tie. In the event of a tie, the pot will be split up by adding up the seeds of the correct picks.

Steps to Play

  • Fill out your contact information using this link.
  • Venmo $5 to @david-mcquillen. Entry fee is due this Friday at noon.
  • If you don’t have Venmo, shoot me a text and we can figure something out.
  • Make your picks using the Pick Submission page.
  • When submitting, your name must match the contact info supplied in Step 1.
  • You can make any number of submissions if you change your mind, up until the tipoff of the first game that day.
  • Once a valid selection has been made, you will see "Pick Submitted" next to your name in the pool.
  • After the cutoff time each day, the final picks for the entire pool will be revealed.
  • Picks are invalid if you have already picked that team, or if the submission timestamp is after the cutoff time.
